Welcome to V3ALab! (Vision, Act, Ask, Answer). Current AI is substantially different from human intelligence in crucial ways because our mind is bicameral: the right brain hemisphere is for perception, which is similar to existing deep learning systems; the left hemisphere is for logic reasoning; and the two of them work so differently and collaboratively that yield creative intelligence.
